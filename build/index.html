OnPlace Inc. bounty campaign

BOUNTY CAMPAIGN TERMS

For a bounty reward will be distributed 1 500 000 OPL

To earn and receive OPL coins, at first you should Log in #OnPlace Inc.. Google Forms. 
After you have connect, just spread information about #OnPlace Inc. ICO in crypto-community as wide as possible by following the simple instructions in this site. 
By completing bounty tasks you will receive bounty-stakes, which are divided in the following categories:

  • twitter;
  • facebook;
  • bitcointalk translation and moderation.

At the end of ICO we will distribute OPL coins proportionally to your bounty stakes in each category.

Facebook bounty campaign

  • Follow our official Facebook page
  • Log in to #OnPlace Inc. form
You repost news marked with #OnPlace_inc or #OPLBounty from our official account within 3 days after their publication and not later, and do not delete them until the PRE-SALE ends. Each repost will earn you the following amount of stakes:
  • From 500 to 999 friends - 10 stakes
  • From 1000 to 1999 friends - 25 stakes
  • From 2000 to 2999 friends - 60 stakes
  • From 3000 to 4999 friends & followers - 120 stakes
  • From 5000 to 9999 friends & followers - 180 stakes
  • More than 10000 friends & followers - 250 stakes

The account must be at least 6 months old and have at least 500 friends. The number of your friends is being fixed at the moment when you connect your account and doesn't change during the campaign. 
NOTE: We count ONLY the posts that are marked with #OnPlace_inc or #OPLBounty by our team. So, if you add our hashtag to any posts, it is NOT counted. 
ATTENTION: We can refuse your participation in bounty campaign without explanation.

Twitter bounty campaign

  • Follow our official page in Twitter
  • Log in to #OnPlace Inc. form
You retweet news marked with #OnPlace_inc or #OPLBounty from our official account within 3 days after their publication and not later, and do not delete them until the PRE-SALE ends. Each retweet will earn you the following amount of stakes:
  • From 500 to 999 followers - 10 stakes
  • From 1000 to 2499 followers - 25 stakes
  • From 2500 to 4999 followers - 50 stakes
  • From 5000 to 9999 followers - 75 stakes
  • From 10000 to 24999 followers - 150 stakes
  • From 25000 to 49999 followers - 200 stakes
  • More than 50000 followers - 250 stakes
The account must be at least 6 months old and have at least 500 followers. The number of your followers is being fixed at the moment when you connect your account and doesn't change during the campaign.
NOTE: We count ONLY the posts that are marked with #OnPlace_inc or #OPLBounty by our team.
So, if you add our hashtag to any posts, it is NOT counted.
ATTENTION: We can refuse your participation in bounty campaign without explanation.

Bitcointalk Translation & Moderation

Apply through this form to book your language ANN translation and thread moderation. 

You will receive 50 stakes for an ANN translation and 5 stakes per page in your thread. 

We offer bounty program participants to implement one or more of the above-mentioned activities in any of the following languages: 

  • Chinese;
  • Japan;
  • Korean;
  • Spanish.

HOW AND WHEN YOU CAN RECEIVE YOUR BOUNTIES 

After the ICO ends, your final amount of stakes will be visible in #OnPlace Inc. statistic table.
We will distribute OPL coins in proportion to the amount of stakes each bounty campaign participant has earned in each category. 
To summarize, the stakes earned in each campaign will NOT be pooled together but rather the coins in each category will be divided in each category separately. 
The information about stakes already earned is displayed in #OnPlace Inc. statistic table. It is updated every 72 hours.
Once all of the stakes are counted, you will see your OPL coins in your Ethereum Wallet.
If you have questions about our bounty campaign, just ask us any convenient way.
